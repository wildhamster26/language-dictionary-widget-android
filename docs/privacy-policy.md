# Privacy Policy for Glance Dictionary

Last updated: June 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Glance Dictionary handles information when you use the app.

## Developer and Contact

Glance Dictionary is provided by Sagy Gideon Mizrahi from Restart Abroad.

For privacy questions or requests, contact us at: sagy@joinrestartabroad.com


## Information We Collect

Glance Dictionary does not collect, transmit, sell, or share user data.

The app does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking SDKs, crash-reporting SDKs, account systems, or server-side storage.

## Information Stored on Your Device

Glance Dictionary stores the information you enter only on your device so the app can provide its vocabulary widget features. This may include:

- Words and translations you add
- Vocabulary categories you create
- Widget display settings, such as font size, column count, selected categories, and collapsed categories

This information is stored locally using Android app storage. It is not sent to the the developer (Sagy), Restart Abroad or to any third party by Glance Dictionary.

Depending on your Android device settings, the Android operating system may back up local app data through system backup features. Any such backup is controlled by your device and Google account settings, not by Glance Dictionary.

## Permissions

Glance Dictionary does not request Android runtime permissions and does not request access to sensitive device data such as location, camera, microphone, contacts, photos, files, SMS, call logs, advertising ID, or health data.

## Third-Party Services and Links

Glance Dictionary includes a user-initiated link to `https://joinrestartabroad.com`. When you tap that link, it opens outside the app through your browser or another app selected by your device. Your use of that external website is governed by that website's privacy practices, not this Privacy Policy.

Glance Dictionary does not embed third-party web content in the app and does not use third-party services to collect data from the app.

## How We Use Information

Because Glance Dictionary does not collect user data, we do not use user data for analytics, advertising, profiling, marketing, or any other external purpose.

Information stored locally on your device is used only to provide app functionality, including displaying your saved words, translations, categories, and widget settings.

## Sharing of Information

Glance Dictionary does not share user data with the developer (Sagy), Restart Abroad, service providers, advertisers, analytics providers, data brokers, or any other third party.

## Data Retention and Deletion

Your words, translations, categories, and settings remain on your device until you delete them in the app, clear the app's storage, or uninstall the app.

Glance Dictionary does not maintain user accounts and does not store user data on developer-operated servers. Because no user data is collected by the developer, there is no server-side user data for the developer to delete.

## Children's Privacy

Glance Dictionary is not designed to collect personal information from children. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if the app's data practices change or to reflect legal or policy requirements. If the app begins collecting, transmitting, sharing, or processing user data beyond local on-device storage, this policy and the Google Play Data safety declarations should be updated before publishing that version.
